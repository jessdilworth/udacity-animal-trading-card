Basset Hound

Basset hounds are very stubborn and hard to train

  • Ears: Their ears are so long to help pick up scents from the ground
  • Legs: Very short despite the fact that they normally weigh between 50-60 pounds
  • Food: They love it
  • Noise: They bay and howl

Basset hounds are funny creatures. They are incredibly stubborn, making them hard to train. Their deep love of food, however, can be used to reward them for good behaviour. They have very long ears and very short legs, which come together to make them one of the best hunting dogs around.
